## Data Sources

- **[rtl_433](https://github.com/mherber/rtl_433)** — 293 protocol signatures for sensors, weather stations, TPMS, meters
- **[Flipper-ARF](https://github.com/D4C1-Labs/Flipper-ARF)** — 30 automotive RF protocols (car keys, gates, garage remotes)
- **[ProtoPirate](https://github.com/D4C1-Labs/ProtoPirate)** — 4 additional automotive protocols (Scher-Khan, StarLine, KIA V2/V3)
- **[HydraSDR](https://github.com/hydrasdr/hydrasdr-host)** — HydraSDR host library and tools
